# botium-toys-internal-security-audit
This repository documents an internal cybersecurity audit conducted for a fictional company, Botium Toys, as part of a professional cybersecurity portfolio. The audit follows the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) and focuses on assessing security controls, compliance gaps, and risk exposure related to business growth, online payments, and international operations.

The project includes a review of the organizationâ€™s IT assets, a risk assessment, and a completed controls and compliance checklist covering administrative, technical, and physical controls, as well as compliance considerations for PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC (Type 1 & 2).

The outcome highlights key security gaps, evaluates potential risks and regulatory exposure, and provides actionable recommendations to improve security posture, protect sensitive data, and support business continuity.
